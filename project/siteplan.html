Nana's Holiday Let Hotel

Site Name: Nanas Holiday Let Hotel

This name reflects a peaceful, elegant, and welcoming hotel experience in Accra. It emphasizes comfort and hospitality, ideal for both tourists and business travelers.

Optional domain: nanasholidaylet.com

Site Purpose

The website will serve as the official online presence of Nana's Holiday Let Hotel. It will provide room details, booking options, amenities, photo galleries, contact information, and local travel tips. The site will also include accessibility features and responsive design for mobile users.

Scenarios

  • How can I book a room online and check availability?
  • What amenities does Nana's Holiday Let Hotel offer for business travelers?

Color Schema

  • Royal Blue (#003366): Used for headers, navigation bar, and call-to-action buttons to convey trust and elegance.
  • Warm Beige (#F5F5DC): Used for background and body text areas to create a soft, welcoming feel.

Typography

  • Playfair Display: Used for headings to give a luxurious, classic look.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text for readability and modern appeal.
